feature realisation of a the abstract catchment specifically as a stream centre line connecting the inflow and outflow of the conceptual catchment its realises. flowpath allows for a straight line realisation.  the flowpath may be represented geometrically as a curve. this concept requires a non-branching mainstem connecting inflow and outflow, and a single linear representation.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>hydrologically this definition refers curve described by a moving particle of water <br /></p><p><i>(Source: path line/flow line.
