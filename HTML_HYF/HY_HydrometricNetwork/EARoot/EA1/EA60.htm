feature realisation of an 'un-realised' catchment specifically as path connecting the inflow and outflow of the logical catchment it realises by following the line of intersection between the water body stratum and the confining depression, or channel. topologically, water edge realises the catchment in terms of an edge connecting two outfalls realised as nodes,  and may be represented geometrically as a curve.  conceptually, a water edge exists with a corresponding flowpath, having nodes fixed at the inflow node and outflow node of the flowpath. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Note: the water edge concept implies a hydrologic complex as a collection of related hydrologic features closed under the hydrologic determination condition in such that if a water edge exists, this edge references the hydrologic determination of the catchment it realises.  <br /></p>
