The <b>HY_CatchmentBoundary</b> feature type realizes an 'un-realized' catchment specifically as catchment boundary connecting the inflow and outflow of the logical catchment it realizes, whereby inflow and outflow may overlay. <br /></p><p>Implying a linear geometric representation, a catchment boundary is topologically understood as an edge bounded by inflow node and outflow nodes, and corresponding to left-bank and right-bank catchment faces inside of the boundary. Hydrologically, the boundary refers to the summit line separating adjacent catchments. HY_CatchmentBoundary inherits from generalization the <i>realizedCatchment</i> association, and carries the properties: <i>shape</i>, and <i>boundaryNetwork</i>. <br /></p>
