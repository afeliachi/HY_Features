layer of consistent characteristics in a body of water (mass of water). A stratum may be determined by differences in thermal or salinity characteristics or by oxygen or nutrient content, but also by virtual storage zones of a reservoir. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Note: This definition is rooted in the definition given in the WMO Glossary of hydrology which defines the stratum as layer within a water body regardless of its location in the respect to the Earth's surface. For the purpose of testing the applicability of the conceptual model in the context of surface water hydrology, the definition of 'stratum' in this standard refers to the zone in a surface water body.<br /></p>
