hydrometric station which is identified part of a network (of at least one station), and may be disjoined into segments. Even if the hydrometric feature may be understood in sense of a sampling feature used for observation, there is no other significance outside the hydrometric network. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>This definition refers to a station at which data on water in rivers, lakes or reservoirs are obtained on one or more of the following elements: stage, streamflow, sediment transport and deposition, water temperature and other physical properties of water, characteristics of ice cover and chemical properties of water.<br /></p><p><i>(Source: station. (2010). In International Glossary of Hydrology Online.Retrieved May 5, 2010, from http://webworld.unesco.org/water/ihp/db/glossary/glu/EN/GF0627EN.HTM)</i><br /></p>
