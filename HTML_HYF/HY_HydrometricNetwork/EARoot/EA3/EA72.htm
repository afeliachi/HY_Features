The HY_RiverReferenceSystem feature defines the catchment-specific linear reference system where the flowpath which realizes the catchment is the linear element forming the axis and the outfall of a catchment forms the origin where the flowpath starts. It is important to note, that the flowpath reflects the topological pattern of a catchment ‘bounded’ by inflow and outflow, and does not necessarily reflect the direction of the natural flow. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>HY_RiverReferenceSystem associates the <i>linearElement</i> and the <i>origin</i>. Constraints <i>start-at-outflow</i>  and <i>start-at-inflow</i> are defined enforcing the start of the flowpath at the inflow or outflow of the realized catchment.<br /></p>
