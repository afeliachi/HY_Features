The <b>HY_HydroNexus</b> feature type (Figure 34) captures the common outlet of a catchment through joining the flow contributed from several catchments, in a single logical ‘junction’ of their separate catchment outlets (Figure 27).  <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>The hydrologic nexus describes the interaction of catchments through connecting contributing and receiving catchments, and the realization of the connected catchments through a ‘fluence’ feature.  Through referencing an already located catchment outlet, a 'fluence' can be identified as (said to be coincident with) the outlet of a connected catchment and may realize that catchment. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>HY_HydroNexus carries the associations: <i>contributingCatchment</i>, <i>receivingCatchment</i>, and <i>nexusRealizationFeature.</i> <br /></p>
