The <b>HY_Flowpath</b> feature type realizes an 'un-realized' logical catchment specifically as a path connecting the inflow and outflow of the catchment it realizes. HY_Flowpath 'drains' a catchment area through connecting flows from atmospheric and groundwater sources to the catchment's common outlet. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>HY_Flowpath specializes HY_CatchmentRealization with respect to an internal catchment topology understanding the flowpath as an edge that forms the lower boundary of the catchment area understood as a face, and is itself bounded by nodes at upstream and downstream ends. Hydrologically, the flowpath is reflects a 'line' describing a moving particle of water. HY_Flowpath may be represented using a linear geometry type.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Through generalization, HY_Flowpath inherits the <i>shape</i> and <i>realizedCatchment</i> properties. It associates the <i>drainedArea,</i> <i>upstreamEnd, downstreamEnd,</i> and <i>flowpathNetwork</i>.<br /></p>
