Each HY_Catchment may provide association between its many realizations within a hydrologically determined hydrologic complex. These realizations include both geometric and topological realizations such as an edge 'bounded' by inflow and outflow nodes. HY_Catchment is an abstract class and may be further specialized with respect to catchment interaction. <br /></p>
