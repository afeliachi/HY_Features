feature realization of an 'un-realized' catchment specifically as a network of connected hydrologic features.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>The HY_HydroNetwork feature type specializes HY_CatchmentRealisation with respect to a network of connected hydrologic features. Such a network realizes in the hierarchical network of logically connected catchments contained in a higher-order catchment. It may be a sequence of flowpaths, an aggregate of water edges, an aggregate of catchment areas or a mesh of catchment boundaries. <br /></p>
