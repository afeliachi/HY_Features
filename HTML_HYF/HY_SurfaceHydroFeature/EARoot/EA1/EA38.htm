feature realisation of an 'un-realised' catchment specifically as a path connecting the inflow and outflow of the logical catchment it realises, incl. a straight line.  a flowpath realises the catchment topologically in terms of an edge connecting two outfalls realised as nodes, and may be represented geometrically as a curve. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Note: the flowpath concept implies a hydrologic complex as a collection of related hydrologic features closed under the hydrologic determination condition in such that if a flowpath exists, this flowpath references the hydrologic determination of the catchment it realises. <br /></p>
