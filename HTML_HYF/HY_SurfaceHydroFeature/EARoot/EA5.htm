The HY_SurfaceWater package summarizes the most common concepts of water bodies on the land surface, each special by the property to move, by origin, by size, by the phase of the contained water, or by their interaction with others. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Concepts may be realized with an identified entity. If required, an application focused on a special type of surface water body may use the concepts provided in this standard to describe the relationship of an implemented concept to the represented catchment. Depending on the particular application, each special type may be described by suitable attributes.<br /></p>
