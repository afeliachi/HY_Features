If the realised catchment is connected with other catchments via outfall, the network channels are also connected.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Note: This definition is rooted in the definition given in the WMO Glossary of hydrology which defines the channel network as an aggregate of natural or man-made channels regardless of their location in respect to the Earth's surface.
