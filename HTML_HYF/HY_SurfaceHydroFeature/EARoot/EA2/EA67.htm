The <b>HY_Flowpath</b> feature type realizes an 'un-realized' catchment specifically as a path connecting the inflow and outflow of the logical catchment it realizes. <br /></p><p>HY_Flowpath specializes HY_CatchmentRealization with respect to an implied linear geometric representation including a straight or curved line. Topologically, the flowpath connects the inflow and outflow of the logical catchment, and is understood as an edge bounded by an inflow node and an outflow node, and corresponding to left-bank and right-bank catchment faces. Hydrologically, the flowpath is a line describing a moving particle of water. Through generalization, HY_Flowpath inherits the <i>realizedCatchment</i> association. It carries the properties: <i>shape</i>, and <i>flowpathNetwork</i>.<br /></p>
