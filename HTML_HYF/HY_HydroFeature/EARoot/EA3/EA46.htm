vertical section of an object along its centre line. the geometric representation (plane or space curves, polygon) may vary with application and needs to be defined there.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Taking into account the separation of a watercourse into water body and confining structure, this concept applies to the (blue) stream line (envelope of the tangents to the instantaneous flow direction at a given time) or stream centre line (connecting the successive centres of cross sections of a stream) as well as to the line following the deepest part of channel (thalweg) and the line of intersection of a water body with the rising land (shore/bank line).<br /></p>
