feature realisation of an 'un-realised' catchment specifically as a contour (line) connecting the inflow and outflow of the logical catchment it realises, incl. a simple polygon.  a contour realises the catchment topologically in terms of an edge connecting outfalls realised as nodes, and may be represented geometrically as a composite curve. if inflow and outflow collaps, the closed contour forms a ring, which may be represented as polygon.
