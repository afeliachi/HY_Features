feature realisation of an 'un-realised' catchment specifically as a catchment boundary connecting the inflow and outflow of the logical catchment it realises. incl. a simple polygon. the catchment boundary realises the catchment topologically in terms of an two edges connecting two outfalls realised as nodes, and may be represented geometrically as a composite curve.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Note: the catchment boundary concept implies a hydrologic complex as a collection of related hydrologic features closed under the hydrologic determination condition in such that if a catchment boundary exists, this is in the same hydrologic complex as the catchment it realises. <br /></p>
