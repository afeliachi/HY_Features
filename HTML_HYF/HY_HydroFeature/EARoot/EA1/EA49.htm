feature realisation of an 'un-realised' catchment specifically as a catchment area connecting the inflow and outflow of the logical catchment it realises, incl. a plane surface. the catchment area realises the catchment topologically in terms of a face connecting the catchment boundary and the draining flowpath each understood as an edge, and may be represented geometrically as a surface. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Note: the catchment area concept implies a hydrologic catchment complex as a collection of related hydrologic features closed under the hydrologic determination condition in such that if a catchment area exists, this is in the same hydrologic complex as the catchment it realises. <br /></p>
