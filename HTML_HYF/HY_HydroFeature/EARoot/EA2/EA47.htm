vertical section of an object at right angles to the main (average) direction of flow. - the geometric representation (plane or space curves, polygon) may vary with application and needs to be defined there.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Taking into account the separation of a watercourse into water body and confining structure, this concept applies to both the (blue) stream cross section at right angles to the main (average) direction of flow, as well as to the transversal bed profile (shape) of a stream bed in a vertical plane.<br /></p>
