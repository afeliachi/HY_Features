The <b>HY_CatchmentDivide</b> feature type realizes an 'un-realized' catchment specifically as boundary line connecting the inflow and outflow of the catchment it realizes. HY_CatchmentDivide encircles the catchment area contributing flows from atmospheric and groundwater sources to the catchment's common outlet. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>HY_CatchmentDivide specializes HY_CatchmentRealization with respect to an internal catchment topology understanding the boundary line as an edge that forms the upper boundary of the catchment area understood as a face, and is itself bounded by nodes such as summit point and pour point. Hydrologically, the catchment divide refers to the summit line separating adjacent catchments. HY_CatchmentDivide  may be represented using a linear geometry type.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Through generalization HY_CatchmentDivide inherits the <i>shape</i> and <i>realizedCatchment</i> properties. It associates the <i>conbtributingArea,</i> <i>summitPoint, poutPoint,</i> and <i>boundaryNetwork</i>.<br /></p>
