body of water, generally flowing in a natural surface channel, incl. subterranean streams. Note: This definition is rooted in the definition given in the WMO Glossary of hydrology which includes water flowing in an open or closed conduit, a jet of water issuing from an orifice, or a body of flowing groundwater. For the purpose of testing the applicability of the conceptual model in the context of surface water hydrology, the definition of 'stream' in this standard refers to water flowing in a surface channel or passing through large interstices, such as a cave, cavern or a group of these.
