These realizations include both topological realizations, as well as their geometric representation. HY_Catchment is an abstract class and needs further specialization with respect to catchment interaction. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>The <b>HY_Catchment</b> feature type specializes the general HY_HydroFeature type. Through generalization, HY_Catchment inherits the <i>name</i> property. It carries a <i>code</i> attribute and the associations: <i>outflow</i>, <i>inflow</i>, <i>containingCatchment</i>, <i>containedCatchment</i>, <i>conjointCatchment</i>, <i>upperCatchment</i>, <i>lowerCatchment</i>, <i>realization</i>.<br /></p>
