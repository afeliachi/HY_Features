The <b>HY_CatchmentOutlet</b> feature type realizes an 'un-realized' logical catchment specifically as a place encompassing the inflow and outflow of the catchment it realizes. HY_CatchmentOutlet 'drains' a catchment area through ewncompassing flows from atmospheric and groundwater sources at the catchment's common outlet. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>HY_CatchmentOutlet specializes HY_CatchmentRealization with respect to an internal catchment topology understanding the outlet as a node that forms the upstream or downstream end on the flowpath, or as the summit or pour point on the drainage divide, both understood as edges.  The zero-dimensional catchment outlet itself has no boundary. Hydrologically, the catchment outlet reflects common outlet towards surface runoff is drained. HY_CatchmentOutfall is geometrically represented using a point shape.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Through generalization, HY_CatchmentOutlet inherits the <i>shape</i> and <i>realizedCatchment</i> properties. It associates the <i>upwardFlowpath,</i> <i>downwardFlowpath, inwardDivide, outwardDivide,</i> and <i>outletNetwork.</i><br /></p>
