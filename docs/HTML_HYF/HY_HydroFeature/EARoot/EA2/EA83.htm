The <b>HY_Flowpath</b> feature type realizes an 'un-realized' catchment specifically as a path connecting the inflow and outflow of the logical catchment it realizes.<br /></p><p>HY_Flowpath specializes HY_CatchmentRealization with respect to an implied linear geometric representation including a straight or curved line. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Topologically, the flowpath connects the inflow and outflow of the logical catchment, and is understood as an edge bounded by an inflow node and an outflow node, and corresponding to left-bank and right-bank catchment faces. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Hydrologically, the flowpath is a line describing a moving particle of water. Through generalization, HY_Flowpath inherits the <i>shape</i> and the <i>realizedCatchment</i> properties. The optional <i>shape</i> of the flowpath is usually a single curve. <br /></p>
