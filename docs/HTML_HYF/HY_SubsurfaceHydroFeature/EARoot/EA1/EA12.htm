water that occurs in the unsaturated zone, e.g. soil water (discharged by evapotranspiration), soil moisture (held by molecular attraction), and gravitational water (moving due to gravity).<br /></p><p><i>(Source: vadose water (2010). In International Glossary of Hydrology Online.Retrieved May 5, 2010, from http://webworld.unesco.org/water/ihp/db/glossary/glu/EN/GF1327EN.HTM)</i><br /></p><p><br /></p><p>This class defines the existence of a water in the zone of aeration (unsatured zone), that may be related to a surface water body by processes of recharge and discharge. To take into account the effects of groundwater interaction on a gaining/losing water body, groundwater may be associated with the relevant water body at the application level.<br /></p>
